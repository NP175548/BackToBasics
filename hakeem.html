Hakeem Olajuwon

The Dream

  1. Hakeem Olajuwon during a game for the Houston Rockets

  • Hakeem Olajuwon won back-to-back NBA championships with the Houston Rockets
  • He was named the NBA Finals MVP both years.
  • Olajuwon is known for his "Dream Shake".
  • He was the first player in NBA history to record over 2000 points, 1000 rebounds, 500 assists, and 200 blocks in a single season.
  • Hakeem was inducted into the Basketball Hall of Fame in 2008.
